Spicy Turkey Tacos

Great way to use all that leftover turkey! Serve tacos with lettuce, shredded cheese, and sour cream.

Ingredients:
  • 1 pound shredded cooked turkey meat
  • 1/3 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 large jalapeno pepper
  • 1 ounce taco seasoning
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 6 small flour tortillas
  1. Combine turkey, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, and taco seasoning mix in a slow cooker; pour in enough chicken broth to almost cover all the ingredients.
  2. Cook on Low for at least 3 hours.
  3. Heat a griddle over medium-low heat and cook tortillas until warmed, about 30 seconds per side. Fill tortillas with turkey mixture.
